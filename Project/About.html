ABOUT US

Indian Railways is India's national railway system operated by the Ministry of Railways. It manages the fourth-largest railway network in the world by size, with 121,407 kilometres (75,439 mi) of total track over a 67,368-kilometre (41,861 mi) route.Forty nine percent of the routes are electrified with 25 KV AC electric traction while thirty three percent of them are double or multi-tracked. Our Railway Mangement System started up in 2017, and we’ve been on an epic ride ever since. From our beginnings as an LPU student in 2017 to launching our first ever railway reservation site in 2018, we’ve tried to stay true to our core beliefs and to deliver an exceptional experience for our community and subscribers for their ease to find there needs. We owe a huge thanks to our community for joining us on this awesome journey, and we hope that you’ll continue to be a part of our story.
